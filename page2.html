This is the second page of a test site, dedicated to teaching.

pulamusic.com is my personal url and website (under construction), meant to be a repository for my work, but more importantly, designed as a place to keep an online teaching portfolio. My intention with this site is to focus on creating and posting teaching materials as multimedia web pages. My plan is to flesh out 4-5 class syllabi, and then produce 2-4 lessons per syllabus, posting them on my site. This way I can work on my coding and web design chops while also - and more importantly - creating a teaching portfolio I can feature online and refer to potential employers.

The syllabi I am currently working on are for four short courses titled:

  • the blues;
  • African American musical aesthetics;
  • early American history; and
  • three Atlantic revolutions: America, France, and Haiti.
The first three courses are based on courses I have already taught, while three Atlantic revolutions: America, France, and Haiti is simply one I would like to teach.
